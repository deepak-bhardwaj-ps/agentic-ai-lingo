A market analysis agent is an AI agent that gathers information about a market and turns it into a short analysis.

In practice, it looks at things like customers, competitors, prices, demand, and trends. It may search the web, read reports, compare sources, and then write a summary or recommendation.

This matters because market decisions are easier to make when the facts are pulled together in one place. A good agent can save time by doing the first pass of research, but a person still needs to check whether the sources are reliable and whether the conclusion makes sense.

It is not the same as a trading bot or stock-picking tool. It is also not a full business strategy team. The agent can help collect and organise evidence, but it does not know the business context, risk appetite, or final goal on its own.
