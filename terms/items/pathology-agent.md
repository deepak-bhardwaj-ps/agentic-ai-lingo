Pathology agent is a specialised AI tool that helps with pathology work.

In practice, it usually looks at digital pathology data such as whole-slide images or pathology reports and helps sort, summarise, or interpret them for a clinician.

It matters because pathology work can involve large numbers of slides and detailed reports. A specialised agent can help a pathologist work faster and keep important findings in view.

It is not a full pathologist, and it is not the same as digital pathology itself. It is also not a fixed standard term. Different companies may use it for slightly different tools, so the exact meaning depends on the product or workflow.
