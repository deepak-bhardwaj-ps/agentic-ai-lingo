DevOps agent usually means an AI tool that helps with software operations work such as checking releases, investigating incidents, and suggesting fixes.

In practice, it reads logs and monitoring data, looks at related systems, and proposes steps a team can take to restore service or prevent the same problem again.

The term matters because it aims to reduce the time people spend on repetitive incident work and speed up response when systems break.

It is not the same as DevOps itself, which is a way of working. It is also not just a chatbot, and it does not replace the engineers who approve, verify, and carry out real changes.
