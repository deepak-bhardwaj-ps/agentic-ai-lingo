A banking agent is an AI agent built for banking tasks.

In practice, it might help a customer check a balance, review recent spending, answer banking questions, or help a bank worker find information faster. Some banking agents can also start or guide actions, but they still need strict safety checks.

The term matters because banking is a high-risk area. A banking agent has to follow rules about privacy, identity, fraud, and money movement.

It is not just a normal chatbot with a finance theme. It is also not a whole banking app, and it is not safe by default. The term is still a bit unsettled, so different companies may use it for slightly different products.
