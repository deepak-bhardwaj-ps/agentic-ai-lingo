Sales agent is software that helps with sales work.

In practice, a sales agent might read lead details, look up customer information, suggest a next step, draft a follow-up message, or help qualify whether a lead is worth more attention. It is usually part of a bigger sales system, such as a CRM tool.

This term matters because sales work often involves many small steps. A sales agent can save time on those steps and help people respond faster.

It is not the same as a human salesperson. It is also not a full sales team, and it should not be treated as safe to contact customers or change records without checks.
