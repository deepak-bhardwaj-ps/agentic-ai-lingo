Medical research assistant is an AI tool that helps people work with medical studies and other health research.

In practice, it helps with things like finding papers, sorting them, pulling out key facts, and making short summaries.

It matters because medical research is slow and full of dense text. A good assistant can save time and help people find evidence faster.

It is not a doctor, and it should not be treated as a replacement for reading the original research or getting expert medical judgement.
