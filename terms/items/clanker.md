Clanker is a rude slang word for a robot or AI system.

In practice, people use it to mock machines that feel cold, noisy, awkward, or like they are replacing human work. It often shows up in jokes, memes, and angry online posts.

The word matters because it shows how some people feel about robots and AI. It is not neutral. It carries attitude, and usually a negative one.

It is not an official technical term. It is also not a precise way to describe one specific kind of machine. If you need clear writing, say exactly what the system is, such as a robot vacuum, chatbot, or customer service bot.
