Healthcare administration agent is an AI agent that helps with non-clinical work in a healthcare setting, such as scheduling, checking details, handling forms, and moving routine requests through a workflow.

In practice, it can gather information, update records, route requests, and hand off harder cases to a person. It is meant to save staff time on office work, not to make medical decisions.

This term matters because healthcare organisations spend a lot of time on paperwork and coordination. A well-built agent can make those tasks faster and more consistent, as long as people still review important actions.

It is not a doctor, nurse, or diagnosis tool. It is also not the same as general healthcare AI, which may help with clinical notes, patient advice, or medical support instead of administration.
