FinRobot is an open-source AI agent platform for finance work, especially equity research and valuation.

In practice, it can gather financial data, analyse it, and turn it into a report. The idea is to help with work that a finance analyst would normally do by hand, such as comparing companies, looking at valuation, and weighing risks.

It matters because finance work often needs several steps, not just one answer. A system like FinRobot tries to organise those steps so the analysis is faster and more repeatable.

FinRobot is not a benchmark, and it is not a general AI chatbot. It is also not financial advice. Its output still needs human checking, especially because finance is high-stakes and the results depend on the data and assumptions used.
