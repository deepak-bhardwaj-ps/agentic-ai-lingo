Cluely is a specific app that listens during a conversation, turns speech into notes, and can suggest answers or follow-up ideas while you are still talking.

In practice, people use it during meetings, interviews, sales calls, classes, or other live conversations. It tries to capture what is being said, write a summary, and help the user respond in the moment.

The term matters because Cluely is not just a normal note-taking app. Its main claim is that it can give live help while staying hidden from other people on the call or screen share.

It is not a general word for all AI meeting tools. It is the name of one product, and the word “undetectable” should not be taken to mean magic or total invisibility in every situation. Its meaning is still tied to a fast-changing product, so the exact features may change over time.
