## Term status

Governance/security concept.

## Meaning

Delegation Audit Trail concerns how an agent's authority, obligations, actions or oversight are defined and evidenced.

## Boundary

It is not a control merely because it is named. Define the accountable principal, permitted scope, enforcement point, audit record, revocation and human escalation.

## How it is used

Delegation Audit Trail is used when authority passes from a person or service to an agent, or between agents. Teams use it to record the originating principal, delegated capability, constraints, expiry, downstream propagation and revocation.
