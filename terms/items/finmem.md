FinMem is a research framework for building an LLM-based trading agent.

In practice, FinMem combines a profile for the agent, a memory system that organises financial information by importance and timing, and a decision step that turns that information into trading choices.

The term matters because it shows one way researchers try to make an AI agent remember useful market facts without treating every piece of information the same.

FinMem is not a general memory feature for all AI systems, and it is not a benchmark by itself. It is a specific research design for financial trading.
