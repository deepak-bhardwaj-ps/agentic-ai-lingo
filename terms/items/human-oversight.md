## Term status

Governance/security concept.

## Meaning

Human Oversight concerns how an agent's authority, obligations, actions or oversight are defined and evidenced.

## Boundary

It is not a control merely because it is named. Define the accountable principal, permitted scope, enforcement point, audit record, revocation and human escalation.

## How it is used

Human Oversight is used to specify where a human reviews, approves, interrupts or overrides an agent. The design must state timing, information provided, authority to act and what happens when no reviewer responds.
