Prompt engineer is a person who specialises in writing, testing, and improving prompts for AI models.

In practice, this means finding the words, examples, structure, and instructions that help a model give a better answer for a specific task.

The job matters because a model does not always guess what you want. A prompt engineer tries to make the request clearer, more reliable, and easier to repeat.

It is not the same as all AI work, and it is not a formal universal job title with one fixed meaning. It is also not enough by itself to make AI safe or correct, because answers still need checking.
