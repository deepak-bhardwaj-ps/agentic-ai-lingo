A supply chain agent is an AI tool built to help with supply chain work, such as planning, logistics, procurement, inventory, or handling changes from suppliers.

In practice, it can answer questions, look up relevant data, check rules, and suggest the next step. Some versions can also help people compare options or spot the impact of a change.

The term matters because supply chain work has lots of moving parts. A good agent can save time and reduce missed updates, but it still needs human oversight for important decisions.

It is not a human supply chain worker. It is not just a chatbot that chats without doing work. It is also not one fixed product name, because different companies use the term for slightly different tools.
