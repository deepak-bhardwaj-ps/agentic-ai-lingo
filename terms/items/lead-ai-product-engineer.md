Lead AI product engineer is a senior hands-on engineer who leads the build of AI features in a product team.

In practice, that means turning a product idea into working software, guiding other engineers, and making sure the AI part is useful, reliable, and shipped well. The person usually works across product thinking, coding, testing, deployment, and review of how the system behaves after release.

The term matters because many AI products now need someone who can connect product goals with technical decisions. It is not just about using an AI model. It is about making the whole feature work in a real product.

This is not a fixed universal job title. Different companies may use it differently. It is usually not a pure management role, and it is not the same as an AI researcher or someone who only writes prompts.
