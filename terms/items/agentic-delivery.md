## Term status

Emerging consultancy label.

## Meaning

Agentic delivery is a loose label for using agents to execute parts of a delivery process such as discovery, implementation, testing or operations.

## Boundary

It is not a defined operating model. Specify the workflow, accountable human, assurance gates and business outcome instead.

## How it is used

Agentic Delivery is used when the emphasis is on getting work to completion rather than merely generating a plan or suggestion. It is most useful when the system has to coordinate people, tools, retries, and verification to finish a job.
