A nurse triage agent is an AI tool that helps nurses sort patient symptoms and decide how urgent the next step should be.

In practice, it may ask questions about symptoms, collect details, suggest whether care can wait, and help route the patient to the right place, such as self-care, a clinic visit, telehealth, or emergency care.

This matters because nurses and care teams often have to make fast decisions from lots of calls or messages. An agent can help organise that work and make the process more consistent.

It is not the same as a full doctor, and it should not be treated as one. It is also not just a general chatbot, because it is meant for a nursing triage workflow with safety rules and human oversight.
