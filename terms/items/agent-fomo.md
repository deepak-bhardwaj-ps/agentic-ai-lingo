## Term status

Informal slang.

## Meaning

Informal online shorthand used in discussion of AI, software or work. Its meaning varies by speaker and has no normative definition.

## Boundary

Do not use it in requirements, governance artefacts or architecture decisions without replacing it with observable criteria.

## How it is used

Agent FOMO is used when teams add agent features mainly to avoid looking behind the market rather than to automate a specific workflow. It usually appears in roadmap debates where the question is not "should we solve this with an agent?" but "can we afford not to have one?".
