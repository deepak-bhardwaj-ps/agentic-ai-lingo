## Term status

Informal slang.

## Meaning

Informal online shorthand used in discussion of AI, software or work. Its meaning varies by speaker and has no normative definition.

## Boundary

Do not use it in requirements, governance artefacts or architecture decisions without replacing it with observable criteria.

## How it is used

Agent FOMO is used as informal, audience-specific commentary on AI culture or working practice. It may be useful for critique, but it should be translated into an observable product, quality, governance or economic claim in professional artefacts.
