Scientific discovery agent is a still-emerging term for an AI agent that helps scientists do research work.

In practice, it may search papers, suggest hypotheses, analyse data, help design experiments, or organise the next research step.

The term matters because science is often slow and full of small steps. A good agent can speed up that work and help people explore more ideas.

It is not a replacement for a scientist. It is also not just a paper-reading chatbot. The best examples still need human judgement, lab checks, and careful review before anyone trusts the result.
