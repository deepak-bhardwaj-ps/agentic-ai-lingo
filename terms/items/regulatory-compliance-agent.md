A regulatory compliance agent is an AI agent that helps check whether work follows laws, rules, and internal compliance policies.

In practice, it may read regulations, compare them with company rules, flag possible problems, and help prepare reports or evidence for review. In stricter settings, it may also screen transactions, documents, or cases and send uncertain ones to a person.

The term matters because regulated work can involve many rules at once, and mistakes can be costly. A compliance agent can help teams notice issues sooner and keep checks more consistent.

It is not a replacement for a lawyer, regulator, or human compliance officer. It cannot guarantee that something is legally safe on its own. It is also not just any chatbot that talks about regulations. It is a specialist agent used to support compliance work.
