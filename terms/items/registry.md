## Term status

Established systems term.

## Meaning

A registry is a catalogue that stores named, versioned artefacts or services so clients can locate them.

## Boundary

In agent systems it may hold tools, skills, agents or [[MCP]] servers; it does not define trust or compatibility without supporting policy and contracts.

## How it is used

Registry is used in discussions of coordination or discoverability between independently built agents and services. Before adopting the label, identify the concrete message format, discovery method, identity exchange, compatibility commitment and failure semantics.
