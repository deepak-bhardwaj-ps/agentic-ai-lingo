Healthcare agentic workflow is a planned sequence of steps where AI helps do healthcare work.

In practice, that usually means the AI helps move a healthcare task forward by reading information, drafting a response, looking up the next step, handing work to the right person or system, and waiting for approval when the task is sensitive.

This matters because healthcare work is full of steps, rules, and hand-offs. A good workflow can reduce paperwork, speed up routine jobs, and help staff keep track of what happens next.

It is not the same as a fully independent doctor or nurse. It is also not just a chatbot answering questions. The main idea is the workflow: the AI is part of a controlled process, usually with human oversight for important decisions.
