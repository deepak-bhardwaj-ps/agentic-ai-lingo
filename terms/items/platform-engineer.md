A platform engineer is an engineer who builds and runs the shared tools and services that other software teams use.

In practice, that means making it easier for developers to create, test, deploy, and operate software without having to know every low-level detail themselves.

The role matters because good platform work can save time, reduce mistakes, and make software safer and easier to support.

It is not just an operations job, and it is not the same as DevOps. The term usually means a person or team focused on improving the developer experience through shared platform services and self-service tools.
