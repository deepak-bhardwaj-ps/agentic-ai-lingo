A solutions engineer is a technical specialist who helps a customer understand whether a product will solve their problem.

In practice, the role usually sits between sales and engineering. A solutions engineer may join customer meetings, answer technical questions, show demos, test ideas, and explain how the product could be set up.

The job matters because many buyers want proof that a product will work for them before they commit. A solutions engineer helps turn a vague promise into something concrete.

This term is not perfectly standard. In many companies it means a pre-sales role. In others it can include support or post-sales help, so the exact work depends on the employer.
