SupportBench is a customer support software platform for B2B teams.

It helps support staff handle tickets, customer details, service-level agreements, escalations, knowledge articles, and reply suggestions in one place.

In practice, a team uses it to see the customer’s history next to each case, route urgent issues, and keep support work organised without jumping between many tools.

This matters because B2B support often depends on account context and response promises, not just answering one question at a time.

SupportBench is not a benchmark, score, or test. It is a product name, and the term is sometimes confused because of the word “bench”.
