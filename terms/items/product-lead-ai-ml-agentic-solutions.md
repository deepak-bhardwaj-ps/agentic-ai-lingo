Product lead, AI/ML agentic solutions is a role for someone who helps choose, shape, and ship AI products that use machine learning and agent-like behaviour.

In practice, this person works with product, engineering, design, and business teams. They decide which problems are worth solving, what the solution should do, and how to measure whether it works.

The term matters because agentic AI products can do more than answer questions. They may plan steps, use tools, and affect real business work, so the product lead has to balance usefulness, safety, and delivery.

This is not a single fixed job title. Different companies use it to mean slightly different things. It is not the same as a machine learning engineer, and it is not just someone who writes prompts or trains models.
