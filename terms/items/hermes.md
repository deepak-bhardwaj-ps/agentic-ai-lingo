Hermes is a personal AI agent from Nous Research.

In practice, that means Hermes is not just a chat box. It can run in a terminal, connect to other chat apps, remember some context across sessions, and use tools and skills to help with tasks.

The term matters because people may use "Hermes" to mean the whole product, while "Hermes CLI" or "Hermes Chat" are just ways of using it.

It is not a general name for every AI assistant. It is a specific product from Nous Research, and the exact features can change as the project grows.
