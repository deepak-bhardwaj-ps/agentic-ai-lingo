## Term status

Engineering shorthand.

## Meaning

Runtime Contract is a proposed explicit agreement about inputs, outputs, permissions, constraints or expected behaviour.

## Boundary

It is not a legal contract or executable guarantee unless expressed and enforced by schemas, policy and tests.

## How it is used

Runtime Contract is used in runtime design to name the component that coordinates decisions and side effects. A useful specification gives its input event, durable state, action contract, retry policy, timeout and terminal states.
