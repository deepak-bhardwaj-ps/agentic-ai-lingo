An AI engineer is someone who builds AI features and systems for software products.

In practice, that usually means working with AI models, connecting them to apps, testing them, putting them into production, and watching how they behave after release.

The term matters because many teams now need people who can turn an AI model into something useful, safe, and maintainable in a real product.

It is not a single fixed job title. People use it in slightly different ways. It is also not the same as a researcher who only studies models, and it is not just prompting a chatbot.
