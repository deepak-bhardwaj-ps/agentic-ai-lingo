An education agent is an AI agent used in teaching, studying, or school administration.

In practice, that can mean a tool that helps a student revise, guides a learner through practice questions, answers questions about course material, or helps a university handle common support tasks.

The term matters because education is a place where AI can be useful, but only if it stays accurate, safe, and easy to supervise.

It is not just any chatbot used in class. If it cannot plan steps, use information, or carry out a task, it is better described as a chat tool or AI tutor, not an education agent.

This is still a loose term. Different schools and vendors may use it to mean different things, so the exact meaning depends on context.
