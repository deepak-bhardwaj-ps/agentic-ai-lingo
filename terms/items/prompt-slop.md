Prompt Slop is a slang term for a prompt that is messy, bloated, copied, or badly organised.

In practice, it means the instructions are harder to use than they should be. A prompt may repeat itself, mix many unrelated ideas, or ask for too much without saying what matters most. That can confuse both the person reading it and the AI model trying to follow it.

The term matters because good prompts need clear goals, simple structure, and enough detail to be useful. A sloppy prompt often leads to sloppy results.

It is not a formal technical measure. It does not tell you exactly how bad a prompt is. It also does not mean every long prompt is bad. A long prompt can still be good if it is clear and useful.
