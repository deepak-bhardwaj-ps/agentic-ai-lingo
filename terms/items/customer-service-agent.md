A customer service agent is an AI system that helps answer customer questions and handle support work.

In practice, it may reply to common questions, ask for missing details, look up account or order information, and pass hard cases to a human support team. Some systems also take small actions, like changing a booking or starting a refund, if they are allowed to do that.

The term matters because customer support has lots of repeated work. A customer service agent can help people get answers faster and reduce the amount of simple work a human team has to do.

It is not the same as a basic chatbot that only follows a script. It is also not the same as a human customer service representative, although some people use the phrase that way outside AI.
