Banking copilot is not a fixed standard term. It usually means an AI assistant that helps with banking tasks for customers or bank staff.

In practice, it may answer banking questions, help complete routine tasks, or guide a person through a banking workflow inside an app or website. Some versions are for customers, while others are for employees doing banking work.

The term matters because banks use these tools to reduce repetitive work and make simple tasks faster. But the bank still needs people, rules, and checks for anything risky, sensitive, or regulated.

It is not the same as a human banker, and it is not just a normal chatbot if it can help carry out real banking tasks. If it only chats and does not help with work, the term is usually too broad.
