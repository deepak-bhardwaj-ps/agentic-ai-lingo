Finance agent is a finance-focused AI agent that helps with tasks such as analysis, reporting, reconciliation, monitoring, and document work in a finance team.

In practice, it usually connects to spreadsheets, finance systems, and other business data, then prepares summaries, flags issues, or helps complete routine steps.

The term matters because finance work has lots of repeated, rule-heavy tasks, but it also needs care, accuracy, and audit trails.

It is not just a normal chatbot, and it is not the same as accounting software or a payment system.

The term is still emerging, so different vendors may use it in slightly different ways.
