## Term status

Governance/security concept.

## Meaning

Behaviour Contract concerns how an agent's authority, obligations, actions or oversight are defined and evidenced.

## Boundary

It is not a control merely because it is named. Define the accountable principal, permitted scope, enforcement point, audit record, revocation and human escalation.

## How it is used

Behaviour Contract is used to document what an agent or delegate is allowed and expected to do. Turn it into a schema, policy and test suite; prose alone cannot constrain an action.
