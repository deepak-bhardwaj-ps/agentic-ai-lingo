Wiki browser is a tool or view for reading wiki pages and moving between them.

In practice, it usually means a page list, sidebar, backlink panel, or visual view that helps you open related pages and follow links through a wiki.

It matters because a wiki is only useful if people can find and move through its pages easily. A good browser makes a wiki feel organised instead of like a pile of disconnected notes.

It is not a web browser, a browser extension, or a search engine. It is also not a fully settled technical term; people use it loosely to describe the part of a wiki or note system that helps you browse pages.
