Page tree is the hierarchy of pages in a wiki or docs site, shown as a tree with parent pages and child pages.

In practice, a page tree helps people browse from a main page down into smaller pages. It often appears in a sidebar or a tree view, where you can expand and collapse sections.

It matters because a page tree helps organise lots of information so people can find what they need without relying only on search.

It is not the same as backlinks, tags, or search. Those help connect pages in different ways. A page tree is about where pages sit in the hierarchy and how they are nested.
