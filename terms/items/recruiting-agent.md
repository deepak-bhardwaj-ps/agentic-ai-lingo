Recruiting agent is an AI agent that helps people with hiring work.

In practice, it may search for candidates, draft messages, screen applications, suggest interview times, or gather hiring notes for a recruiter to review.

It matters because recruiting often has a lot of repetitive work. A recruiting agent can save time and help teams move faster, but it still needs clear rules and human oversight.

It is not the same as an applicant tracking system, which mainly stores and organises hiring records. It is also not the final hiring decision-maker. People should still check the output, especially because hiring decisions can affect fairness and may be covered by employment law.
