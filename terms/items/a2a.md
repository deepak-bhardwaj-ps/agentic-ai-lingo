## Term status

Established protocol.

## Meaning

An open protocol for agent-to-agent communication. It defines discovery, capability advertisement, task lifecycle, messages, artefacts and streaming between independently built agents.

## Boundary

It is not a multi-agent framework and does not specify an agent's reasoning loop, tools or safety policy.

## How it is used

A2A is used in discussions of coordination or discoverability between independently built agents and services. Before adopting the label, identify the concrete message format, discovery method, identity exchange, compatibility commitment and failure semantics.

[Google, Agent2Agent protocol](https://google.github.io/A2A/) provides the relevant primary source or established reference. For coined labels, it is background for the underlying concept—not evidence that the label itself is standard.
